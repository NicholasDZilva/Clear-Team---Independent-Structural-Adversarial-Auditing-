# Clear Team — Independent Structural Adversarial Auditing

**Clear Team** is a conceptual framework developed by Invariant Systems for **Independent Structural Adversarial Auditing** of AI-assisted decision systems and organizational architectures.  

This repository contains:

- Conceptual descriptions of the **Invariant Architecture** framework
- Guidelines for structural invariant definitions and governance layers
- High-level principles for **structural auditing** and **decision integrity assessment**
- Comparative overview of Clear Team vs Red Team methodologies

**Key Principles:**

1. **Focus on Architecture, Not Outputs**  
   Clear Team audits what a system *cannot do* structurally, rather than only what went wrong in outputs.

2. **Preservation of Structural Invariants**  
   Decision integrity, solution space coverage, actor autonomy, reasoning traceability, and authority scope are maintained across variance types (environmental, adversarial, operational, temporal).

3. **Independent, Adversarial Assessment**  
   Audits must be external to the deploying organization to avoid framing capture, expertise inversion, and recalibration conflict.

4. **Two-Tier Invariant Classification**  
   Hard invariants are unconditional; soft invariants allow bounded operational flexibility. Violations of hard invariants signal architectural failure.

5. **Audit Without Disclosure**  
   Conceptual audit patterns are observable without exposing proprietary algorithms, scoring functions, or internal mechanics.

## **Usage**

This repository is intended for **educational and conceptual purposes**. It is **not an implementation**. Proprietary frameworks, measurement methods, and operational mechanics are deliberately omitted.  

Organizations or researchers may:

- Reference the Clear Team methodology conceptually

- Explore governance and architecture principles for AI and complex decision systems
